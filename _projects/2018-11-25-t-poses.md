Leading up to the release of *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate* on the Nintendo Switch, I started a project of custom 3D renders in Blender of all the characters in the *Smash Bros.* games I had played in the past. This helped build the excitement for the new game among the *Smash* community, but also pushed me to what I was capable of in Blender at the time: Model cleanup, posing, hair and particle simulation, compositing, and rendering.
