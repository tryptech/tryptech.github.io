The Filipino Intercollegiate Networking Dialogue’s Third District runs a contest every year for a new logo/t-shirt design. I entered for the 2013-2014 year and won.

This was the first time I had done any work with printing in mind. The contest limited color palettes to black-on-white or white-on-black with one accent color. I used halftone to subvert the one color limit and achieve a tint of the accent color to give the design some depth.
